Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about using Collabuu for businesses, influencers, and customers.

All General Business Influencer Customer Technical Payment

Collabuu is a platform that connects businesses with influencers and customers through QR code campaigns, token-based payments, and loyalty reward systems. We help businesses increase foot traffic, influencers earn money for promoting local businesses, and customers discover deals while earning loyalty rewards.

Collabuu works in four simple steps:

  • Businesses create campaigns with token budgets and requirements
  • Influencers apply to campaigns and create content with unique referral codes
  • Customers discover deals, generate QR codes, and visit businesses
  • System automatically rewards all parties when QR codes are scanned at businesses

The Collabuu app is free to download and create an account. However:

  • Businesses pay only for results through our token system
  • Influencers earn money from successful campaigns
  • Customers use the app completely free and earn rewards

Our pricing is transparent and results-based:

  • Pay Per Customer: $5-15 per verified visit
  • Pay Per Post: $25-100 per approved content post
  • Media Events: $500+ for large coordinated campaigns
  • Platform Fee: 5% of all transactions

You only pay when you get results. Unused tokens are fully refundable within 30 days.

Creating a campaign is easy:

  • Set up your business profile with photos and information
  • Choose your campaign type (Pay Per Customer, Pay Per Post, etc.)
  • Define your target audience and requirements
  • Set your budget and token allocation
  • Review and publish your campaign

Influencers can then discover and apply to your campaign.

Visit verification is automatic and secure:

  • Customers generate unique QR codes from the app
  • You scan the QR code using the Collabuu business app
  • The system verifies location and campaign eligibility
  • Credits are automatically transferred to the influencer
  • Customer receives loyalty points

You have 24 hours to confirm or dispute visits.

Getting started as an influencer is simple:

  • Download the Collabuu app and create an account
  • Complete your influencer profile with social media links
  • Verify your social media accounts and follower counts
  • Browse available campaigns in your area
  • Apply to campaigns that match your audience

You don't need millions of followers - micro-influencers are welcome!

Earnings vary based on campaign type and performance:

  • Pay Per Customer: $5-15 per customer visit you generate
  • Pay Per Post: $25-100 per approved content post
  • Media Events: Share of $500+ campaign pools
  • Loyalty Campaigns: Ongoing commissions from repeat customers

Top influencers earn $500-2000+ per month depending on their engagement and local market.

Content requirements vary by campaign, but typically include:

  • Social posts on Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, or Facebook
  • Stories featuring the business or product
  • Reviews on Google, Yelp, or other platforms
  • Blog posts or articles about your experience
  • Video content for YouTube or TikTok

All content must include proper FTC disclosure and follow platform guidelines.

Customers get multiple benefits:

  • Discover deals from local businesses through influencer content
  • Earn loyalty points every time you visit participating businesses
  • Redeem rewards like discounts, free items, or exclusive offers
  • Support local businesses and your favorite influencers
  • Find new places to eat, shop, and explore in your area

Using QR codes is simple:

  • Find a business deal through an influencer's content
  • Tap the "Get Deal" button in the Collabuu app
  • A unique QR code is generated for you
  • Visit the business and show your QR code
  • The business scans it and you automatically earn points
  • Check your rewards balance and redeem when ready

Collabuu is available on:

  • iOS: iPhone and iPad running iOS 14.0 or later
  • Android: Devices running Android 8.0 (API level 26) or later
  • Web: Modern browsers including Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge

The app works best with a camera for QR code scanning and location services for visit verification.

QR code issues are usually due to:

  • Expired codes: QR codes expire after 24 hours for security
  • Location mismatch: You must be at the business to scan
  • Campaign ended: The campaign may have ended or budget exhausted
  • Already used: Some codes can only be used once
  • Poor lighting: Ensure good lighting for camera scanning

Try generating a new QR code or contact support if issues persist.

Our payment system is secure and transparent:

  • Businesses purchase tokens using credit cards or bank transfers
  • Tokens are held in escrow until campaign completion
  • Payments are automatically released when conditions are met
  • Influencers receive payments via direct deposit or PayPal
  • Processing takes 7-14 business days

All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Our refund policy is designed to be fair to all parties:

  • Unused tokens: Fully refundable within 30 days of purchase
  • Active campaigns: Can be cancelled with unused tokens refunded
  • Completed campaigns: Non-refundable as influencers have already been paid
  • Disputed results: Reviewed case-by-case with potential partial refunds
  • Processing time: 5-10 business days to original payment method

Influencer payment schedule:

  • Campaign completion: Payment triggered when campaign goals are met
  • Verification period: 24-48 hours for business to confirm results
  • Processing time: 7-14 business days for bank transfer
  • Minimum payout: $25 USD (earnings below this accumulate)
  • Tax reporting: 1099 forms issued for earnings over $600 annually
