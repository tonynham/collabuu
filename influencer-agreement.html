Influencer Partnership Agreement

Last Updated: June 2025

This Influencer Partnership Agreement ("Agreement") outlines the terms and conditions between you ("Influencer," "Creator," or "you") and Collabuu Inc. ("Collabuu," "we," "us," or "our") for participating in promotional campaigns through the Collabuu platform ("Platform").

1. Overview and Benefits

By using the Platform and applying for campaigns, you agree to the terms in this Agreement.

Key Benefits for Influencers

  • Access to sponsored campaigns from local businesses
  • Performance-based earnings and bonuses
  • Streamlined content submission and approval process
  • Real-time analytics and transparent payment tracking
  • Professional networking opportunities with verified brands

2. Eligibility and Account Requirements

2.1 Minimum Requirements

  • 18+ years of age (or legal guardian consent)
  • Active social media presence with public-facing content
  • Accurate representation of engagement metrics and audience
  • Geographic eligibility based on campaign region

2.2 Verification

  • Government-issued ID (if requested)
  • Verification of social media handle
  • Payment method verification
  • Tax documentation if applicable (e.g., T4A, 1099, or W-8BEN depending on country)

3. FTC and Disclosure Compliance

You must disclose paid partnerships in accordance with FTC and Canadian advertising laws.

3.1 Required Disclosures

  • Hashtags: #ad, #hosted or otherwise specified by the business
  • Stories: Use platform-native paid promotion labels
  • Verbal disclosure for video content within first 30 seconds

3.2 Placement Rules

  • Clear, unambiguous, and before any CTA
  • Cannot be buried or hidden in a long list of hashtags
  • In the same language as the post

Failure to disclose sponsorships correctly may result in campaign suspension or legal consequences.

4. Campaign Participation

4.1 Application & Briefing

  • Apply to campaigns via Platform
  • Upon approval by the business, receive a brief with creative requirements

4.2 Content Guidelines

  • Post within 7–14 days or as specified by the business
  • Use high-quality visuals
  • Authentic and professional tone
  • Include CTAs and unique QR/tracking codes

4.3 Approval Process

  • Users must submit a link to the content through the Platform

Content must stay live for the campaign's minimum duration.

5. Payment & Taxes

5.1 Payment Structure

  • Pay Per Customer: Commission for verified customer conversions
  • Bonuses (if applicable): Based on campaign goals or long-term loyalty

5.2 Payouts

  • Processed in 7–14 business days
  • Minimum payout threshold: $25 CAD

5.3 Tax Responsibilities

You are responsible for reporting your earnings to your local tax authority. Collabuu:

  • Issues T4A forms for Canadian influencers (CAD $500+ annually)
  • Issues 1099 forms for U.S. influencers ($600+ USD annually)
  • May require W-8BEN for international participants

Consult a tax professional for accurate filing.

6. Content Ownership and Rights

6.1 Ownership

  • You retain ownership of original content
  • You grant Collabuu and campaign businesses a 12-month non-exclusive license to use content for marketing, reposts, and advertisements

6.2 Restrictions

  • Cannot sell content to competitor brands
  • Cannot delete approved content before the expiration period
  • Cannot modify posts to remove sponsor-related disclosures

7. Campaign Performance & Metrics

  • Use unique tracking codes or QR links
  • Share engagement metrics upon request (e.g., screenshots, story views)
  • Maintain performance standards:
    • Timely delivery
    • Clear communication
    • Accurate reporting

Repeated failure to meet performance standards may affect future campaign access.

8. Prohibited Behavior

  • Fake followers, likes, comments, or engagement
  • Fraudulent QR scans or customer visits
  • Misrepresentation of account stats
  • Contacting businesses outside of Platform to bypass process
  • Promoting competitors during an active campaign

Violations may result in account suspension or payment forfeiture.

9. Termination

9.1 By Influencer

  • You may terminate this Agreement with 7 days' written notice
  • All in-progress campaigns must be completed
  • Granted usage rights remain in effect for 12 months post-termination

9.2 By Collabuu

We may suspend or terminate your account for:

  • Repeated disclosure violations
  • Fraud or manipulation
  • Legal/regulatory non-compliance

10. Support & Resources

  • Dedicated influencer success team
  • Creative briefs and platform walkthroughs
  • Tax guides and payout FAQs

Top influencers may receive exclusive campaign access, performance-based bonuses, and personalized coaching.

11. Legal

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Province of Alberta, Canada. Any disputes shall be resolved in Alberta courts.

12. Contact

Influencer Support: support@collabuu.com

Campaign Help: support@collabuu.com

Payment Issues: support@collabuu.com

Legal: support@collabuu.com

This Influencer Agreement is designed to create successful partnerships between content creators and local businesses while ensuring compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.
