Collabuu Advertiser Agreement

Last Updated: June 2025

This Advertiser Agreement ("Agreement") sets forth the terms and conditions between you ("Business," "Advertiser," or "you") and Collabuu Inc. ("Collabuu," "we," "us," or "our") regarding your use of the Collabuu platform ("Platform") to promote your business through influencer campaigns and customer engagement initiatives.

1. Overview

By creating a business account and posting campaigns on Collabuu, you agree to be bound by this Agreement in addition to the Collabuu Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Key Benefits for Businesses

  • Access to verified local influencers and content creators
  • Pay-only-for-results pricing model
  • Real-time customer tracking through QR code technology
  • Automated payment processing and campaign management
  • Comprehensive analytics and ROI tracking

2. Business Eligibility & Account Setup

2.1 Requirements

  • Must be a legally registered business entity
  • Authorized representative must accept these terms
  • Must provide accurate contact and billing details
  • Responsible for maintaining your account security

2.2 Verification

  • Collabuu may request proof of business registration or tax ID
  • Campaigns may be subject to manual review before launch

3. Token Purchase and Use

3.1 Token System

  • Tokens are used to fund influencer campaigns and customer rewards
  • Minimum purchase amount: $25 CAD
  • Tokens have no cash value and are non-transferable between accounts

3.2 Token Refund Policy

  • Unused tokens are non-refundable
  • Tokens allocated to active or completed campaigns are non-refundable

3.3 Fees and Charges

  • Platform fee: XX% of all token transactions
  • Stripe or third-party payment processing fees apply

4. Campaign Management

4.1 Campaign Creation

  • Define campaign type (Pay Per Post, Pay Per Customer, Event, Loyalty, etc.)
  • Set content requirements, creative guidelines, and payment structure
  • Provide campaign assets if applicable (e.g., logo, brand messaging, links)

4.2 Influencer Applications

  • Review and approve influencer applicants
  • Provide timely feedback and approvals (within 48 hours recommended)

4.3 QR Code Management

  • Collabuu provides unique QR codes for tracking
  • Businesses must scan codes through the Platform app
  • Confirmation of visits must be completed at time of visit via scanning customer QR code
  • Fraudulent scanning or bypassing the system may result in penalties

5. Campaign Types

5.1 Pay Per Customer

  • Triggered by verified QR scans from customer visits
  • Business must scan customer QR code at time of visit

5.2 Pay Per Post

  • Influencers submit content for approval
  • Content must remain live for required duration

5.3 Media Events

  • Shared credit pool across influencers
  • Based on participation and performance metrics

5.4 Loyalty Reward Advertising

  • Businesses define point values and redemption rules

6. QR Code Usage

  • QR codes are unique campaign identifiers
  • May include time or usage restrictions
  • Only valid when scanned via the Collabuu app
  • Fraudulent use may result in penalties

7. Payments to Influencers

  • Tokens are automatically deducted from your balance when influencers complete deliverables or drive verified results
  • Payouts to influencers are managed by Collabuu; no direct payments should be made outside the platform
  • Disputed conversions or deliverables must be reported within 48 hours of completion for investigation

8. Business Responsibilities

  • Accruately Scan QR codes upon customer visit
  • Ensure your products/services are accurately represented
  • Provide legitimate and timely customer experiences
  • Comply with all applicable advertising, tax, and consumer protection laws
  • Not misuse the Platform for spam, misinformation, or fraudulent promotions

9. Prohibited Conduct

  • Attempting to bypass the platform to engage influencers directly
  • Falsifying scan data, customer actions, or influencer contributions
  • Reposting influencer content without approval or outside allowed terms
  • Using Collabuu to promote prohibited products or services (e.g., illegal drugs, adult content, weapons)

10. Content and Brand Usage

  • Businesses receive a limited, non-exclusive license to use influencer-generated content for 12 months
  • Content may be used for advertising, reposting, and promotional use in accordance with campaign terms
  • You may not alter required disclosures or remove branding unless approved by the creator

11. Data and Privacy

  • Businesses only receive influencer handles and campaign deliverables
  • No access to personal information or platform analytics beyond your own campaign
  • All user data is protected in accordance with Canadian privacy laws (PIPEDA)

12. Termination

12.1 By Business

  • You may close your account at any time
  • Refunds for unused tokens are governed by the token refund policy
  • Existing campaigns will continue unless explicitly cancelled

12.2 By Collabuu

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate business accounts for:

  • Fraudulent behavior or false advertising
  • Failure to meet campaign obligations
  • Breach of this Agreement or Terms of Service

  • We are not responsible for the quality or outcome of influencer content
  • You are solely responsible for your business's compliance with local laws
  • To the maximum extent permitted by law, Collabuu shall not be liable for indirect or consequential damages

14. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Province of Alberta, Canada. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Alberta.
